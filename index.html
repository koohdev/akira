In creating Akira, I aimed to construct a visually immersive and meticulously detailed world.

Every element, from the characters to the cityscapes, reflects a commitment to realism and a deep consideration of the impact of technological and societal changes on the environment and individuals.

Kaneda embodies the spirit of resilience and rebellion.

designing Kaneda, I aimed to create a character who personifies the strength to confront adversity, the courage to challenge authority, and the tenacity to navigate a world in turmoil.

His philosophy is rooted in the belief that even in the face of chaos and uncertainty, one can find purpose and meaning through personal conviction and the bonds formed with others.

"Akira" is a 1988 anime film that explores themes such as power, corruption, destruction, rebirth, individual vs. society, technology and humanity, and existential questions.
